The ErgoDox project has inspired several popular variants and derivatives. While they share the core columnar stagger 
design, they each offer unique improvements such as pre-assembled convenience, concave key wells, or enhanced 
connectivity.

On the following sections, we explore the most notable ErgoDox variations:
- **ErgoDox EZ**: The most popular pre-assembled, commercial version.
- **ErgoDox Infinity**: A high-tech version with LCD screens and USB-C.
- **Dactyl**: A 3D-curved, concave variant for maximum ergonomics.
